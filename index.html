About

Hello There, I'm an industrial designer based in Crossett, Arkansas, recently graduated from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette with a minor in Moving Image Arts. My work includes digital CAD modeling and hands-on fabrication.

My capstone project, the Formlabs Mold, is a desktop injection molder concept inspired by the Formlabs line of products.
